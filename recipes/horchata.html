Horchata

What Is Horchata?

Horchata is a Mexican dairy free drink that is typically made with rice, water, cinnamon, milk, and sugar.

How To Make Horchata?

Here is a list of Ingredients to make the best Horchata.

Horchata Ingredients:

  • 2 quarts of hot water
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup of long grain rice
  • 1 cup of milk
  • ice cubes, for serving
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions

Step 1

Pour sugar, cinnamon, and rice into hot boiling water; star until sugar dissolves. Cover, and refrigerate for at least 3 hours. For better taste let it sit overnight in refrigerate.

Step 2

Pour the rice mixture into a strainer over a pitcher of choice and discard the rice.

Step 3

Stir in the milk, vanilla, cinnamon, and sugar. Refrigerate for 1 hour.

Step 4

Stir well before serving and serve over ice.
