Steak Burrito Recipe

Steak Burrito

How To Make A Steak Burrito

We will introduce you to make the best Steak Burrito you will ever make. This meal is quick and easy to make with every bite bringing a tender and juicy steak.

Steak Burrito Ingredients

  • 1(10-inch)flour tortilla
  • 3/4 cup cubed, cooked ribeye steak(Season and cook to preferences), warmed
  • 1/4 cup mexican or white rice, heated
  • 1/4 cup pinto beans, heated
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon diced onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon diced tomato
  • Sauce of choice or guacamole click here to learn how to make.

Directions

Step 1

Heat flour tortilla on pan or grill till warmed. Plate tortilla and spread beans and rice onto tortilla. Optional: Spread guacamole onto flour tortilla before placing beans and rice.

Step 2

Add your steak followed by cheese, onions, cilantro, and tomato. Spread ingredients flat across your tortilla.

Step 3

Fold over two opposite ends of flour tortilla and keep those ends folded over in place; while you bring one of the ends of your burrito over the filling and begin to roll and tucking until all the fillings are enclosed.

Step 4

Optional: Cut burrito in half for better serving.
