Guacamole

What Is Guacamole?

Guacamole is a popular Mexican dish that traditional consist of avocados, onions, cilantro, and lime juice. This dish is a perfect party snack with studies also showing benefits to health such as heart, skin, and hair.

How To Make Guacamole?

Before we move on, it is important to have all the basic ingredients to make the best guacamole dish.

Guacamole Ingredients:

  • 3 avocados- peeled & mashed
  • 1 whole lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 rome tomatoes, diced cut
  • 1/2 cup of diced cut onions
  • 1 jalapeno, diced cut
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • 1 cilantro, chopped

Directions

Step 1

Add the 3 peeled avocados into a bowl or molacjete, mash the avocadoes then add lime juice, salt, and garlic; and stir together.

Step 2

Mix in tomatoes, onions, jalapeno, and cilantro into the bowl.

Step 3

Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours for improved flavor.
